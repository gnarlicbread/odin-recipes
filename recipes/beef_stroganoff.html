Beef Stroganoff

This simple Beef Stroganoff recipe is rich, creamy, and easy to make! Great for busy weeknights! Ready to serve in under 30 minutes

ingredients

  • 1 (8 ounce) package egg noodles
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 (10.5 ounce) cam fat-free condensed cream of mushroom soup
  • 1 tablespoon garlic powder, or to tast
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • salt and ground black pepper to tast
  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Saute ground beef in a large skillet over medium heat until
    browned and crumbly; 5 to 10 minutes.
  3. Meanwhile, fill a large pot with lightly salted water and bring to a
    rapid boil. Cook egg noodles at at boil until tender yet firm to the
    bite, 7 to 9 minutes. Drain and set aside.
  4. Drain and discard any fat from the cooked beef. Stir condensed
    soup and garlic powder into the beef. Simmer for 10 minutes,
    stirring occasionally.
  5. Remove beef from the heat. Add egg noodles and stir to combine.
    Stir in sour cream and season with salt and pepper.
  6. Serve hot and enjoy!
